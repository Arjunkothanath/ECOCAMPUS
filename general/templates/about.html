About CampusEcoTrack

CampusEcoTrack is a smart waste management system for educational campuses.

It helps track bin status, allows students and staff to give feedback, and promotes sustainability through organized waste collection and monitoring.

Our goal is to build a cleaner, smarter campus through eco-friendly digital solutions.
